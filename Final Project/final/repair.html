Repairing Your Collectibles

Be prepared when the worst happens!
If it's broken right out of the box, definitely contact the seller or manufacturer and try to get a replacement.
However, if it's an easy fix, there are various adhesives on the market and easily available with online web retailers these days to make repairs relatively painless.
This video offers a quick rundown, and is a great resource for any collector.



Insuring Your Collectibles

Insuring your collectibles is a personal choice, but here at Culture Collectibles we feel like your treasured collectibles deserve to be protected.
Insuring collectibles is also often more affordable than you'd think, typically only a few dollars a month.
Check with your property insurer to see their options.
