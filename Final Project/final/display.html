Displaying Collectibles

You've bought some collectibles, but don't know how to display them properly?
The good news is there is no right or wrong way to display your collection!
While it may seem like a daunting and potentially expensive prospect, it doesn't have to be.
You can display your collectibles however you please - on bookshelves, coffee tables - basically any sturdy, flat surface can be used to display your stuff.
