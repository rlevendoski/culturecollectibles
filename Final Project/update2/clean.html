Cleaning & Care

Cleaning and caring for your collectibles is usually pretty simple, especially when you make it routine.
Here are some great options for cleaning as well as tips for general care.

Cleaning Care
Micofiber Cloth:This is your best bet for most collectibles.
  • Do not display collectibles in direct sunlight. Doing so will cause sun damage or bleaching.
    		•
    Air: This can be done with Air in a Can or a vacuum with the reverse suction feature.
    *Great for Lego sets!
  • Make sure all shelves or pedestals are secure, with wall anchors or other safety mechanisms.
    		•
    Damp Cloth: Carefully wipe down any tough-to-remove dust or dirt using a damp cloth. A mild soap or cleaner can also be used for extra tough grime.
    *Use your common sense with this and do not use water on anything that can be damaged by moisture.
  • Be careful displaying items in high-traffic areas, especially if they have elements that stick out. These can easily snag on you/your clothing as you walk by. This includes areas frequented by pets.
    		•
    Goo Gone: This is great for removing any sticky substance, such as residue from stickers.
    *A little bit goes a long way, so don't overdo it. Great for book covers.
  • Keep collectibles out of reach of pets. Keep your pets natural behaviors in mind. Climbing, scratching, chewing, scenting, and marking are just a few natural pet behaviors that can be damaging.
