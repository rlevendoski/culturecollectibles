Storage & Relocation

  • Culture Collectibles recommends keeping all original packaging for your collectibles. It makes storage and relocation incredibly easy and offers peace-of-mind.

  • If you've already disposed of the original boxes, bubble wrap, air pouches, and tissue paper can work though they are obviously not ideal.

  • Ensure the collectibles are wrapped securely and into a box, ideally a perfect fit for the collectible, to avoid needing to much packing material. Do not place multiple fragile collectibles in the same container.

Lego-Specific Storage & Relocation

  • It's best to store Lego in an air-tight bin with the directions and any extra pieces in a plastic bag, stapled or taped to the instructions.

  • If you're trying to store or relocate an assembled set and do not have an air-tight tub, I recommend sealing the set in a plastic bag and then setting the entire thing in a cardboard box larger than the set. Seal up all the corners with tape to avoid losing any tiny pieces.

  • Please keep in mind that while moving an assembled set, unless you're going a very short distance and can keep an eye on the tub or box, it is likely some pieces will break off.
